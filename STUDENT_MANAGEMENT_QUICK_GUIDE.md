# Quick Reference: Student & Subject Management

## 🚀 Quick Start for Different Roles

### 👨‍💼 Admins
You have full access to everything!

#### Edit a Student
1. Go to **Student Management**
2. Find the student in the table
3. Click the **blue pencil icon** (Edit)
4. Choose tab: **Student Information** or **Manage Subjects**
5. Make changes and click **Save**

#### Assign Subjects to a Student
**Method 1 - Quick (Subjects Only)**
- Click the **purple book icon**
- Select/deselect subjects
- Click **Save Subjects**

**Method 2 - Full (Edit Everything)**
- Click **blue pencil icon**
- Go to **Manage Subjects** tab
- Select subjects + set academic year/term
- Click **Save Subjects**

#### Assign Subjects to Entire Class
1. Click **Bulk Assign Subjects** button (top right)
2. Select the class
3. Select subjects
4. Confirm - all students in class get these subjects

#### Delete a Student
1. Click the **red trash icon**
2. Confirm deletion

---

### 👨‍🏫 Form Teachers
You manage students in your assigned class(es).

#### What You Can Do
✅ Edit student personal information  
✅ Add new students to your class  
✅ Manage subjects for your students  
✅ Delete students from your class  

#### What You CANNOT Do
❌ Edit students in other classes  
❌ Add students to other classes  

#### Edit a Student in Your Class
1. Go to **Student Management**
2. Your class is already selected
3. Find the student
4. Click **blue pencil icon**
5. Edit or manage subjects as needed

#### Add New Student
1. Click **Add Student** button
2. Your class is pre-filled
3. Fill in student details
4. Optionally select subjects for enrollment
5. Click **Create**

---

### 📚 Subject Teachers
You manage which subjects students take.

#### What You Can Do
✅ Manage subjects for secondary students  
✅ See which subjects each student has  

#### What You CANNOT Do
❌ Edit student personal information  
❌ Delete students  
❌ Add new students  

#### Assign Subjects to a Student
1. Go to **Student Management**
2. Click **blue pencil icon** on student
3. Go to **Manage Subjects** tab
4. Search and select subjects
5. Set academic year/term
6. Click **Save Subjects**

#### Quick Subject Management
1. Click **purple book icon** on student
2. This is a faster interface for just managing subjects

---

### 🎓 Form + Subject Teachers
You have all the above capabilities!

---

## 🎯 Common Tasks

### Search for a Student
1. In **Student Management**, look for the **Search box**
2. Type name, parent email, or registration number
3. Press Enter

### Filter by Class
1. Use the **Class** dropdown
2. Select your class or "All Classes"

### Filter by Level
1. Use the **Level** dropdown
2. Choose: Nursery, Primary, Secondary, Pre-Nursery

### Export Student List
1. Click **Export** button (top right)
2. File downloads as CSV
3. Open in Excel

---

## 📋 Subject Management Details

### Finding Subjects
- **Search box** - Search by name or subject code
- **Categories** - For Secondary (SSS), subjects group by: Science, Art, Commercial
- **Auto-filter** - Only shows subjects for student's level

### Academic Settings
- **Academic Year** - Usually current year (e.g., 2024)
- **Term** - First, Second, or Third Term
- **Notes** - Optional notes about why subject was assigned

### Selected Subjects Count
- Shows **"Save Subjects (3)"** if 3 subjects selected
- Button disabled until at least 1 subject selected

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- **Changes save immediately** - No draft mode
- **Student info and subjects** - Save separately
- **Cannot undo deletion** - But you can re-add student
- **Permissions are enforced** - Disabled buttons mean you don't have access

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Button is Disabled"
- Check your role/permissions
- Make sure you're not trying to edit another teacher's class
- Admins should always see all options

### "Student Not Found"
- Use the Search box
- Check the filter (Level, Class)
- Make sure student exists

### "Subject Not Found"
- In Subject Management, try searching
- Check student's level matches subject level
- For SSS students, may be in a different category

### "Changes Not Saved"
- Check for error messages
- Verify all required fields are filled
- Try again - may be temporary issue
- Check your internet connection
